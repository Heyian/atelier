# Atelier vocabulary

- **Skill** — a capability you add to Claude by uploading a ZIP file. Claude
  fires it on its own when the situation matches; you never have to name it.
- **Role skills** — skills built for one role or department: marketing, sales,
  meetings, and any you create with `atelier-forge`. An open-ended set.
- **Core skills** — the fixed foundation that serves every role: `atelier`
  (the hub), `atelier-mentor`, `atelier-compass`, `atelier-forge`.
- **Company Profile** — the document describing your role, company, offer,
  market, tone of voice, priorities, team, vocabulary, and AI ambitions. Lives
  at `{root}/docs/atelier/company-profile.md`.
- **Root** — the single folder where everything about your project lives.
  Established during the onboarding interview.
- **Role registry** — the index of your role skills, at
  `{root}/docs/atelier/roles.md`.
- **Relay (relais)** — the handoff document that lets you continue your work in
  a new conversation without re-explaining everything.
- **Compass** — the thinking process for decisions and initiatives too fuzzy to
  simply execute.
- **Decision log** — the dated record of your decisions and why you made them,
  at `{root}/docs/atelier/decisions.md`. Each entry reads on its own and is
  never edited.
- **Corporate memory** — everything Atelier carries from one session to the
  next: the decision log plus living state (profile, role memories, progression,
  registry).
- **Workspace** — a Claude Project dedicated to one department, with its own
  instructions and reference documents.
- **Routine** — recurring work Claude runs without you, on a fixed schedule.
